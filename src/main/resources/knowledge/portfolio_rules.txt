PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT RULES & CONSTRAINTS

ASSET LIMIT RULES:
- Maximum 5 assets per category for NSE (Equity) and MF (Mutual Fund)
- Maximum 3 assets per category for COMMODITY
- These limits prevent over-diversification and maintain focus
- Exceeding limits requires selling existing positions first

DIVERSIFICATION PRINCIPLES:
- Spread investments across at least 2-3 asset categories
- Avoid putting more than 50% in any single category
- Within categories, diversify across different assets
- Balance between diversification and concentration for optimal returns

RISK MANAGEMENT RULES:
- Monitor portfolio beta weekly
- Review concentration risk monthly
- Alert on single asset > 20% of portfolio
- Alert on category > 50% of portfolio
- Alert on commodity exposure > 30%

PERFORMANCE MONITORING:
- Review portfolio performance monthly
- Compare against benchmark (NIFTY50 for Indian markets)
- Track unrealized vs. realized P&L separately
- Monitor top and bottom performers regularly

REBALANCING GUIDELINES:
- Rebalance when allocation drifts > 10% from target
- Consider tax implications before selling
- Maintain target beta range based on risk tolerance
- Rebalance gradually, not all at once

PORTFOLIO CONSTRUCTION BEST PRACTICES:
- Start with asset allocation target (e.g., 40% NSE, 30% MF, 30% Commodity)
- Build positions gradually
- Review and adjust quarterly
- Keep cash reserve (5-10%) for opportunities

COMPLIANCE & CONSTRAINTS:
- No automatic trade execution (manual approval required)
- All trades must be reviewed by portfolio manager
- Maintain audit trail of all decisions
- Document rationale for significant changes

CLIENT-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS:
- Risk tolerance varies by client
- Investment horizon affects asset selection
- Tax considerations impact trading decisions
- Client goals determine portfolio strategy

MONITORING CHECKLIST:
- Weekly: Check portfolio value, unrealized P&L
- Monthly: Review allocation, concentration risk
- Quarterly: Full portfolio review, rebalancing assessment
- Annually: Comprehensive performance analysis
